About NeuralBridge

The NeuralBridge 2025 is a workshop uniting Neurotechnology, AI, and Clinical Innovation. It serves as a bridge between disciplines, where AI researchers, clinicians, and students disseminate and exchange knowledge to advance the science and practice of brain–computer interfaces (BCI) and brain health technologies.

Beyond keynotes and demonstrations, the workshop features a hands-on BCI competition, where participants design solutions to decode neural signals. By combining rigorous science with creative exploration, the NeuralBridge Workshop aims to bridge thought and technology, inspiring the next generation of innovations at the frontier of neural intelligence and clinical translation.

Call for participation

We invite students, researchers, clinicians, and innovators to take part in this hands-on journey into the future of neurotechnology.

Join us for engaging talks, live demonstrations, and a BCI competition, where participants will have the chance to design intelligent systems that read, interpret, and respond to brain signals. Whether you are new to the field or an experienced practitioner, the NeuralBridge Workshop offers an opportunity to learn, collaborate, and help shape the next generation of AI-driven brain health technologies.
